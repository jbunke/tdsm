This is a rough outline of planned features for upcoming updates:

- Pokemon Trainer [Gen 4]:
  - More animations: Run, item interactions, ...
  - More customization options: body & head shapes, hairstyles, clothes, accessories, ...
  - More layers: facial hair
  - Separating components onto multiple layers to allow for more nuanced layering

- More sprite styles
  - Style inspired by Stardew Valley
  - Original 16- and 32-bit styles with 2, 4, 6, and 8 directions

[Stretch Goals]
- Define your own sprite style by uploading assets and scripts with composition logic
- Sprite styles with portraits
