You can buy/download more sprite styles for TDSM online.
Sprite styles approved by the developer (either made by
the developer or community members) are compiled in the
collection linked below.

Note that some sprite styles feature assets adapted from
or inspired by established IPs, and that the use of such
sprites in commercial software may infringe the copyright
of the original owners.

Please visit the "Tutorials" page to learn how to make
sprite styles yourself.

You can upload your original sprite styles to itch.io and
post their link in the forum linked below to be featured
in the developer-approved collection of sprite styles.
